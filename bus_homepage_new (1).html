OUR MOTIVE

Traveling in Bangalore is a can get really stressful. Thanks to the local Bus service, BMTC for making available thousands of buses for the general public. But these buses have thousands of series of numbers and are almost always confusing to any person.Though mybmtc.gov.in did fairly well, it had its own limitations. But now we are here bringing in this beautiful piece of website to find the routes accurately and conviniently.Find out more about our vision , team and updates here.
